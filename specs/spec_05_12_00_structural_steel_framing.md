# SECTION 05 12 00 - STRUCTURAL STEEL FRAMING

## PART 1 - GENERAL
* **Scope**: This section specifies structural steel framing, including fabrication, erection, and high-strength bolting.

## PART 2 - PRODUCTS
### 2.1 Materials
* **Structural Steel Shapes (W-shapes)**: ASTM A992, Grade 50 (50 ksi yield strength).
* **Channels, Angles, and Plates**: ASTM A36 (36 ksi yield strength).
* **High-Strength Bolts**: ASTM F3125/F3125M, Grade A325, Type 1.
* **Anchor Bolts**: ASTM F1554, Grade 36 or Grade 55.

### 2.2 Connection Design
* **Bolted Connections**: High-strength bolts must be installed in snug-tight or slip-critical connections as indicated.
  * Slip-critical connections must be tightened using the **turn-of-nut method** or tension-control bolts.
  * Clean faying surfaces of slip-critical connections; do not paint unless using qualified slip-resistant paint.

## PART 3 - EXECUTION
### 3.1 Erection tolerances
* **Plumbness**: Individual columns must be plumb within **1:500** (approx. 0.25 inches in 10 feet) up to a height of 60 feet.
* **Alignment**: The alignment of members must be within **1/4 inch (6 mm)** of the project datum.
* **Welding Compliance**: Erection welding must comply with **AWS D1.1/D1.1M Structural Welding Code - Steel**. All welders must be certified for the specific positions and processes used.
